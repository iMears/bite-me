What is Bite Me?

  • A voice-controlled kitchen helper app that makes cooking from a recipe a hands-free experience.

How to use Bite Me:

  • Find and select a recipe
  • Make sure your volume is turned up
  • Press the "Start Cooking" button
  • Allow Bite Me to access your microphone by selecting "allow" in the top right corner of your browser
  • Talk to Caesar using the provided commands
  • Have fun cooking with Caesar!

Caesar Commands:

  • "Next step" - Get next step in recipe
  • "Repeat step" - Get last step in recipe
  • "How much (flour)" - Get quantity of given ingredient
  • "Set timer for (10) minutes" - Set timer for given duration
  • "Goodbye Caesar" - End cooking instructions
  • "Caesar tell me a joke" - Easter egg
  • "Caesar help" - Prompt Caesar for help
